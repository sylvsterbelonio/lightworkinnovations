  1. Information We Collect
    • Information You Provide
      • Account Information:

        When you register for an account, we may collect personal information such as your name, organization code, email address, and password.

      • Profile Information:

        The user can input basic information such as details (nickname, birthday, gender, marital status, etc.), upload photos and cover photos, family information, work background, address, social information, and contact information. The main purpose is to allow or share the user with other fellow Christian friends, pastors, mentors, and church ministries who were using our products. But these features are optional only; the user has the right not to fill up the information to protect his/her privacy rights.

      • Daily Devotional:

        Our product collects data information for the daily reflection in God's word. But this feature does not need to require any users to input their thoughts and ideas. The main purpose is to help keep track and store the data information and allow him/her to share with his/her mentor, fellow Christians, and ministries about his/her reflection. It also gives inspiration and motivation to anyone who reads his/her thoughts for reflection in God's word.

      • Home Cell Monitoring:

        Our product has special features to allow the user to record the household information after he/she conducts a bible study inside the house, church, or any place where they are gathered. The purpose is to record and track the progress of what topic they are covered for the next upcoming lessons. This also allows them to share from their fellow Christians, mentors, network team, and even the church ministry to help monitor and analyze the data so that they could provide a better way or method to increase the productivity and performance. These are the following information we collect but are not limited to: photos, date gathered, time gathered, name, leader, host, timothy, material, lesson, household name, address, household members basic information (name, gender, category, role, contact no., and type), and remarks. But this feature is not necessary to force the user to input the information. This is optional only. 

      • Ministry:

        This feature allows the user to join a ministry; the account and personal information of the user will automatically be shared with the ministry for the purpose of statistics report, analytics, performance, productivity and monitoring. This will allow the user to be notified about the events, meetings, trainings, anniversary celebrations, special gatherings (regional, national, and international), and Sunday gatherings. And to encourage the user to participate in any events conducted by the church ministry. But this product feature does not require any user to join the ministry; it depends upon the decision of the users.

      • Network:

        "Network" means a small group of Christian people who gather in order to create a strong bond in the relationship of Christ. They are helping each other by mentoring, teaching the word of God, walking a true life as Christians, and serving the Lord wholeheartedly. 

        It divides into two categories: mentor and team network. 

        1. When the user has no mentor, our products will encourage him/her to find it. The purpose is to help the user learn the basic foundation of Christ through the knowledge of his/her mentor by following the proper steps and guidelines. The "mentor" has a right to use his personal information for the purpose of recording, tracking, monitoring, and analyzing the performance, productivity, and lessons covered. 
        2. When the user had learned the foundation of Christ, graduated in different trainings, and became fully equipped with knowledge and skills. He/she may allow to form a group of network teams. He will become a mentor to those people who are non-believers (new users) and will begin to teach them by following the right system. He/she may now have a right to use the personal information for the purpose of recording, tracking, monitoring, and analyzing the performance, productivity, and lessons covered. 

        REMINDERS: This feature does not need to force the user to join the network; it still depends upon the user's decision.

      • QR Code:

        This feature will allow the user to view other users's information by scanning the QR code with the same product they used. Other codes beyond our products will not be recognized. He/she also allows to share his/her information with other users's by sharing his/her QR code. The other purpose is to use as an attendance checker if the user did participate in the events in the ministry. But this is not required to force the user to use this special feature.

        This feature will allow the user to interact with other users by commenting and liking other posts. It collects the account information, such as the user ID, name, email address, and photos, of the users and will be shared with other users of our products. Any information outside of this feature will never be shared. But this feature does not need the user to use it.
