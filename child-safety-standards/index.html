Safety Standards & Contact (LPZ System)

All apps in the Social or Dating category must publish safety standards and contact details to comply with our Child Safety Standards Policy.

Our Safety Standards

  • Age limits: Social: 13+; We verify age and remove underage accounts.
  • Protection of minors: Strictly bans grooming, sexual content involving minors, exploitation, and harassment.
  • Moderation: Proactive detection, human review, and swift removal/ban for violations.
  • Reporting tools: In-app report/block on every profile and message; urgent reports prioritized.
  • Parental/guardian controls: Guidance for minors; education on safe use.
  • Data privacy: Minimal data collection; secure storage; no sale of children’s data.
  • Law enforcement: We cooperate with lawful requests and escalate imminent-harm cases.
